Tilt-based scrolling

Using a phone or other gyroscope-enabled device, try tilting your phone forward to scroll down the page.

What's going on here?

Tl;dr: We're accessing your the accelerationIncludingGravity property of your devices gyroscope and translating it's z coordinate to scroll the document.

When accessing gyroscope data from the browser for the purpose of providing a constant force on an object (in this case, the window), we put our focus on device acceleration as it provides an. The title is slightly misleading
